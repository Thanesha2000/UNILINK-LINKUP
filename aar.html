Welcome to AARZOO!

THE DRAMA SOCIETY OF SRM

AARZOO, the drama society of SRM, is dedicated to fostering the creative expression of students through acting, theater, and performance arts. Our society brings together passionate individuals who wish to explore the world of drama and develop their theatrical skills.
