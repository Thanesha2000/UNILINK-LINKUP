Welcome To HACKHOUND!

THE TECH REVOLTUION OF SRM

Welcome to HackHound, the dynamic technical society of SRM! From immersive workshops and round-the-clock hackathons to hosting SIH internal hackathons, we ignite innovation and foster a community of tech enthusiasts eager to push boundaries and create solutions for tomorrow. Our mission is to empower students with hands-on experience, cutting-edge technologies, and collaborative platforms where creativity meets technical expertise. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a curious beginner, HackHound provides a thriving ecosystem to grow, learn, and transform ideas into impactful realities. Join us on our journey to drive the future of tech!
